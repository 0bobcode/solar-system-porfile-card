MERCURY

First Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Orbit: 87.97 days
  • Surface: Rocky
  • Interesting Fact: No atmosphere

VENUS

Second Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Orbit: 224.70 days
  • Surface: Hot and Volcanic
  • Interesting Fact: Thick, toxic atmosphere

EARTH

Third Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbit: 365.25 days
  • Surface: Water and Land
  • Interesting Fact: Only planet known to support life

MARS

Fourth Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Orbit: 687 days
  • Surface: Red and Rocky
  • Interesting Fact: Once had liquid water

JUPITER

Fifth Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Orbit: 11.86 years
  • Surface: Gas Giant
  • Interesting Fact: Largest planet in the solar system

SATURN

Sixth Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 116,460 km
  • Orbit: 29.46 years
  • Surface: Gas Giant with Rings
  • Interesting Fact: Iconic ring system

URANUS

Seventh Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 50,724 km
  • Orbit: 84.01 years
  • Surface: Ice Giant
  • Interesting Fact: Rotates on its side

NEPTUNE

Eighth Planet from the Sun

  • Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Orbit: 164.79 years
  • Surface: Ice Giant
  • Interesting Fact: Strong winds and a dark storm called the Great Dark Spot
